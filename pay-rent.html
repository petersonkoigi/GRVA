To complete your payment, please follow the instructions below:

  • Step 1: Open your M-PESA app (or any other payment platform that supports Paybill).

  • Step 4: Enter the Account Number: 748693
  • Step 5: Enter the amount you want to pay (in KES).
  • Step 6: Confirm and send the payment.
